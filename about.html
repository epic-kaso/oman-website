Who we are & what we do

OMAM NIGERIA LIMITED (A member of OMAM GROUP was registered on 9/3/2012 at Corporate Affairs Commission Abuja with registration No 1016765 as a private limited liability company. Our company has franchise for importation and distribution of quality HP, Samsung and Dell computers, photocopiers, printers and their consumables, office and educational equipment.

We have in stock large quantities of branded laptops and desktop, brand new printers and photocopying machines, flash drives and hard disks, coloured and black inks, different types of toner cartridges and anti-virus among others. We also distribute educational, hospital and office equipment. Our other subsidiaries are: OMAM REAL-ESTATE and OMAM CONCRETE INDUSTRY.

Our Vision

In the near to be one of the foremost real estate developers and to have the franchise from our suppliers. For establishment of Ict gadgets and office/educational and hospital equipment assembling plant in Nigeria.

Our Mission

To be one of the best importers of quality information technology gadgets and office, educational and hospital equipment in Nigeria through the engagement of qualified personnel to ensure customer optimal satisfaction and return of dividend to our stakeholders. And also to assist various tiers of government in their various mass housing programe.
